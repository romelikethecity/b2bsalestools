What is B2B Sales Tools?

B2B Sales Tools are software platforms that help sales teams find prospects, manage outreach, close deals, and coach reps.

The modern B2B sales stack spans everything from contact databases and email sequencing to conversation intelligence and revenue forecasting. Choosing the right combination of tools directly impacts pipeline velocity, win rates, and rep productivity.
