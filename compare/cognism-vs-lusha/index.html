Choosing between Cognism and Lusha is one of the most common decisions teams face in the B2B Data category. Both platforms have loyal users, and both have real trade-offs that don't show up on feature comparison charts. This breakdown covers pricing, usability, integrations, and real-world fit so you can skip the sales demos and make a faster call.

It's about which workflow your team actually runs day to day.

For most teams evaluating B2B Data tools in 2026, Lusha is the safer starting point. It scores 7.5/10 overall and starts at Free / $29/mo. That doesn't mean Cognism is the wrong choice. Teams that need need GDPR-compliant data will find it's a better fit.

Which Is Right for Your Stage?

Startups & SMBs

Lusha is the better fit here. It starts at Free / $29/mo and won't require a dedicated admin to manage. Teams under 20 reps rarely need the extra horsepower Cognism offers.

Growth Stage

This is where the decision gets harder. Both tools work at this scale. Evaluate based on your specific workflow: if your team prioritizes us data, lean toward either option. Run a two-week trial with both if you can.

Enterprise

Cognism typically wins at enterprise scale, where customization, integrations, and admin controls matter more than sticker price. But get a custom quote from both vendors. Enterprise pricing is always negotiable.

Questions to Ask Before Choosing

  1. What's the total cost for my team size? Both Cognism and Lusha have pricing that scales differently. Get a custom quote, not just the published rate.
  2. Which CRM does my team use? Integration depth varies. One of these tools will connect more cleanly with your existing stack.
  3. How many reps will use this daily? Some tools charge per seat, others by usage. The answer changes the math at 10 reps vs 50 reps.
  4. Can I get a trial with my real data? Feature comparisons on paper miss how a tool actually feels in your workflow. Run both for a week if possible.
  5. What does the contract look like? Ask about auto-renewal terms, cancellation windows, and whether you're locked into an annual commitment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Lusha better than Cognism?

For most teams, yes. Lusha scores 7.5/10 vs 8.1/10 in our evaluation. Lusha wins for US-focused teams on a budget. Cognism wins for EMEA data quality and GDPR compliance. If you sell into Europe, pay for Cognism. Everyone else should start with Lusha.

How much does Cognism cost vs Lusha?

Yes. Most teams complete the migration in 2-4 weeks. The biggest effort is moving sequences and templates. Export your data from Cognism, map the fields, and import into Lusha. CRM integrations may need reconfiguration.
