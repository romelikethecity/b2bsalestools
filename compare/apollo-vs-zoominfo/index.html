Which Is Right for Your Stage?

Startups & SMBs

ZoomInfo is the better fit here. It starts at $14,995/yr and won't require a dedicated admin to manage. Teams under 20 reps rarely need the extra horsepower Apollo.io offers.

Growth Stage

This is where the decision gets harder. Both tools work at this scale. Evaluate based on your specific workflow: if your team prioritizes data accuracy, lean toward the higher scorer. Run a two-week trial with both if you can.

Enterprise

Apollo.io typically wins at enterprise scale, where customization, integrations, and admin controls matter more than sticker price. But get a custom quote from both vendors. Enterprise pricing is always negotiable.

Questions to Ask Before Choosing

  1. What's the total cost for my team size? Both Apollo.io and ZoomInfo have pricing that scales differently. Get a custom quote, not just the published rate.
  2. Which CRM does my team use? Integration depth varies. One of these tools will connect more cleanly with your existing stack.
  3. How many reps will use this daily? Some tools charge per seat, others by usage. The answer changes the math at 10 reps vs 50 reps.
  4. Can I get a trial with my real data? Feature comparisons on paper miss how a tool actually feels in your workflow. Run both for a week if possible.
  5. What does the contract look like? Ask about auto-renewal terms, cancellation windows, and whether you're locked into an annual commitment.
