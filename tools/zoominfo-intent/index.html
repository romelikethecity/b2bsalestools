The Bottom Line

ZoomInfo Intent is the path of least resistance for teams that already use ZoomInfo and want to add intent data to their prospecting workflow. Bombora data quality is proven, and the native integration eliminates the setup cost and ongoing management of a separate intent tool.

The decision gets more nuanced for teams evaluating from scratch. ZoomInfo Advanced (contact data + intent) often costs less than standalone ZoomInfo Professional + standalone Bombora. But 6sense's blended intent data and buying stage predictions provide a richer signal for teams running ABM programs. The right choice depends on whether your primary need is sales prospecting (ZoomInfo wins) or ABM orchestration (6sense wins).

Buy ZoomInfo Intent if you're already a ZoomInfo customer or if you need both contact data and intent signals in a single platform with minimal setup. Buy standalone Bombora if you need granular topic control and already have a separate data provider. Buy 6sense if you need intent data wrapped in a full ABM platform with predictive analytics.

ZoomInfo Intent Overview

ZoomInfo Intent packages Bombora Company Surge data directly inside the ZoomInfo platform. Instead of buying standalone Bombora and integrating it yourself, ZoomInfo customers on Advanced and Elite plans get intent signals surfaced in the same interface they use for prospecting, enrichment, and workflow automation.

The integration is the value proposition. Intent signals show up alongside contact data, company profiles, and engagement tools. A sales rep can see that a target account is surging on a relevant topic, find the right contacts at that account, and add them to an Outreach sequence without leaving ZoomInfo. That workflow consolidation is worth a lot to teams that don't have RevOps resources to stitch together multiple tools.

The intent data itself is Bombora's Company Surge product. The same topic taxonomy, the same cooperative data model, the same surge scoring methodology. ZoomInfo doesn't add proprietary intent signals the way 6sense does. What you gain is convenience and workflow integration. What you lose is the granular topic control and custom segmentation available in standalone Bombora.

For teams already paying for ZoomInfo, adding intent through the Advanced plan is usually the most cost-effective way to get Bombora data. The incremental cost is significantly less than a standalone Bombora contract. For teams evaluating intent data from scratch, it's worth comparing the all-in cost of ZoomInfo with intent versus standalone Bombora plus a separate data provider.

Reps can identify surging accounts, find contacts, and launch outreach in a single workflow. No integration work, no separate logins, no data stitching.
  • Powered by Bombora (industry-standard data)ZoomInfo Intent uses Bombora Company Surge data, the most widely adopted intent dataset in B2B. The data quality is proven and the topic taxonomy covers 12,000+ topics. You're getting the same core data that standalone Bombora customers pay $25K+/year to access.
  • Lower cost than standalone Bombora for ZoomInfo customersIntent data is included in ZoomInfo Advanced ($24,995+/year) and Elite plans. The incremental cost of adding intent to an existing ZoomInfo contract is significantly less than purchasing standalone Bombora ($25K+ separately). For existing ZoomInfo customers, this is the obvious choice.
  • Automated workflow triggersZoomInfo lets you set up automated workflows triggered by intent signals. When a target account surges on a relevant topic, the system can automatically add contacts to sequences, update CRM records, or notify reps via email/Slack. This turns intent data from a passive report into an active prospecting trigger.
  • Requires ZoomInfo subscription as baselineYou can't buy ZoomInfo Intent standalone. It's an add-on to ZoomInfo's data platform, which starts at $15K+/year. If you don't need ZoomInfo's contact data, the all-in cost for intent access alone is much higher than standalone Bombora.

  • Intent features vary by plan tierBasic ZoomInfo plans don't include intent data. You need Advanced or Elite. The jump from Professional to Advanced can be $10K+ per year. Make sure you're evaluating the total cost including the plan upgrade, not just the intent add-on price.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ZoomInfo Intent the same as Bombora?

ZoomInfo Intent uses Bombora Company Surge data as its underlying intent source. The data methodology and quality are the same. ZoomInfo adds workflow integration, automated triggers, and native access within the ZoomInfo platform. Standalone Bombora offers more granular topic control and custom segmentation options.

Which ZoomInfo plan includes intent data?

Intent data is available on ZoomInfo Advanced and Elite plans. Professional and lower plans do not include intent. Advanced starts around $24,995/year. The upgrade from Professional to Advanced is typically $10,000-$15,000/year depending on your existing contract terms.

How many intent topics can I track?

ZoomInfo Advanced customers can typically track 25-75 topics, depending on their contract. Elite customers get access to broader topic coverage. Standalone Bombora offers more topic flexibility. For most use cases, 25-50 well-chosen topics provide sufficient coverage to identify in-market accounts.

Can I use ZoomInfo Intent without the rest of ZoomInfo?

No. ZoomInfo Intent is an integrated feature, not a standalone product. You need an active ZoomInfo subscription on the Advanced or Elite tier. If you only need intent data, standalone Bombora is the more economical choice.

How does ZoomInfo Intent compare to 6sense?

ZoomInfo Intent provides Bombora-based intent data integrated into a prospecting platform. 6sense provides blended intent data (Bombora + proprietary) integrated into an ABM orchestration platform with buying stage predictions. ZoomInfo is better for sales-focused teams that need data + intent in one tool. 6sense is better for ABM teams that need intent + orchestration + advertising.
