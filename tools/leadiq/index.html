The Bottom Line

LeadIQ is the best tool for SDRs who prospect primarily through LinkedIn. The one-click capture from LinkedIn profiles to CRM is the fastest workflow in the category, and it saves meaningful time for reps who spend hours per day in Sales Navigator. Job change alerts surface high-converting outreach opportunities that most teams miss entirely. And the $39-$79/month per-user pricing makes it affordable for SDR teams to adopt across the board.

The tool's scope is intentionally narrow, and that's a strength. LeadIQ doesn't try to be a bulk enrichment platform, an AI research engine, or a multi-provider waterfall tool. It does one thing exceptionally well: help reps go from LinkedIn profile to qualified CRM lead in seconds. Teams needing broader enrichment capabilities should pair LeadIQ with Clay or FullEnrich for bulk list processing and use LeadIQ exclusively for real-time LinkedIn prospecting.

Buy LeadIQ if your SDRs spend significant time in LinkedIn Sales Navigator and you want to maximize the number of qualified contacts they capture per hour. The Pro plan is worth the $40/month premium over Essential if job change tracking and AI personalization are valuable to your team. Skip LeadIQ if you primarily prospect from databases, build lists in bulk, or if you need waterfall enrichment for maximum coverage.

LeadIQ Overview

LeadIQ is built for one specific workflow: an SDR browsing LinkedIn, finding a good prospect, and capturing their contact data with a single click. The Chrome extension sits on top of LinkedIn and Sales Navigator, letting reps grab verified email addresses and phone numbers from prospect profiles and push them directly into their CRM and sequencing tools. No copy-pasting, no tab-switching, no manual data entry. That single-click capture workflow is LeadIQ's core value, and the company has refined it over years.

The platform goes beyond basic data capture. LeadIQ tracks job changes across your saved contacts, alerting you when a champion or prospect moves to a new company. These job change signals are among the highest-converting triggers in B2B sales. A former champion who just started as VP of Sales at a new company already knows your product, already trusts you, and is actively building their tech stack. LeadIQ surfaces these opportunities automatically and gives you the new contact data to reach them.

LeadIQ's Scribe feature uses AI to generate personalized email copy based on the prospect's LinkedIn profile, company, role, and recent activity. The quality is solid enough for first-touch outreach, producing personalized openers and value propositions that are noticeably better than generic templates. For SDR teams sending 50+ emails per day, Scribe saves 30-60 minutes of writing time while maintaining a level of personalization that improves reply rates.

The limitation is clear: LeadIQ is a LinkedIn prospecting tool that includes enrichment, not a dedicated enrichment platform. If your reps live in LinkedIn Sales Navigator and need to capture contact data as fast as possible, LeadIQ is the best tool for that workflow. If you need bulk enrichment for 5,000-contact lists, waterfall enrichment across multiple providers, or enrichment of data from sources beyond LinkedIn, tools like Clay, FullEnrich, or Apollo serve those use cases better. LeadIQ excels in a narrow lane and doesn't pretend otherwise.

Pros & Cons

  • Fastest LinkedIn-to-CRM capture workflow availableOne click on a LinkedIn profile captures the prospect's verified email, direct phone, title, company, and LinkedIn URL, then pushes it to Salesforce or HubSpot as a new lead and to Outreach or Salesloft as a sequence step. The speed eliminates 15-20 minutes per hour of prospecting that SDRs spend on manual data entry and tool-switching. Over a full week, that's 5-8 hours saved per rep.
  • Job change tracking that surfaces warm outreach opportunitiesLeadIQ monitors all saved contacts for job changes and sends Slack or email alerts when someone moves to a new role. Job change outreach converts to meetings at 3-5x the rate of cold outreach because the prospect already has a relationship with you. The tracking runs automatically across all saved contacts. A team with 500 saved champions can expect 5-15 job change alerts per month, each one a warm pipeline opportunity.
  • AI email personalization with ScribeScribe analyzes a prospect's LinkedIn profile, company news, and role to generate personalized email copy including subject lines, openers, body text, and calls to action. The output quality is strong for first-touch outreach. SDRs who write 50+ emails per day save 30-60 minutes while sending messages that feel individually crafted. Scribe doesn't replace a skilled seller's writing, but it raises the floor for the entire team.
  • Affordable per-seat pricing that scales with SDR teamsThe Free plan includes 20 verified emails per week. The Essential plan at $39/month per user adds CRM integration and higher limits. The Pro plan at $79/month per user adds Scribe AI, job change alerts, and expanded credits. A 10-person SDR team on Essential pays $390/month total for LinkedIn prospecting with CRM sync. That's less than one month of Clay's Starter plan.
  • Single-source data with no waterfall enrichmentLeadIQ uses its own database for contact enrichment. If LeadIQ doesn't have a prospect's email or direct dial, there's no fallback to other providers. Waterfall tools like Clay and FullEnrich try multiple sources in sequence and deliver measurably higher coverage. LeadIQ's email find rate runs 60-70% on average, while waterfall approaches hit 80-90%. For every 1,000 prospects, that's 100-200 additional contacts you're missing.
  • Chrome extension dependency creates fragilityLeadIQ's primary interface is a Chrome extension running on LinkedIn. LinkedIn periodically changes its page structure, which can temporarily break the extension. Chrome updates sometimes cause compatibility issues. The browser-based approach also means slower performance during heavy prospecting sessions with many open tabs. Some users report conflicts with other LinkedIn extensions like Sales Navigator's own tools.
  • Limited bulk enrichment capabilitiesLeadIQ is optimized for one-at-a-time prospecting from LinkedIn profiles. The platform supports CSV uploads for bulk enrichment, but this isn't its strength. Processing 5,000 contacts through LeadIQ's bulk enrichment is slower and provides less coverage than running the same list through Clay, FullEnrich, or Databar. Use LeadIQ for real-time LinkedIn capture and a dedicated enrichment tool for list processing.
  • Scribe AI varies in quality by prospect profileScribe generates strong personalization for prospects who have detailed LinkedIn profiles with recent posts, activity, and clear company descriptions. For prospects with sparse profiles (no photo, no posts, minimal experience section), Scribe's output is generic and requires significant editing. The quality ceiling is high but the floor depends on the prospect's LinkedIn presence.

Use Cases

SDR Team Prospecting from LinkedIn Sales Navigator

An SDR runs an advanced search in Sales Navigator for VP of Marketing at SaaS companies with 200-1,000 employees in the US. They browse through results, evaluating each profile for ICP fit. When they find a strong prospect, one click on the LeadIQ extension captures the prospect's verified email, direct phone, title, company, and LinkedIn URL. The data pushes automatically to Salesforce as a new lead and to Outreach as a step in a multi-touch sequence. LeadIQ's Scribe generates a personalized first line referencing the prospect's recent LinkedIn post about their team's Q4 marketing strategy. The SDR adds 30-35 qualified prospects to their pipeline in a 2-hour session, work that would take an entire day with manual research and data entry.

Sales Team Capitalizing on Champion Job Changes

A 15-person sales team has closed 300 deals over the past 4 years. Each closed-won contact is saved in LeadIQ's tracking system. LeadIQ monitors all 300 champions for job changes and sends Slack notifications to the original account executive when a champion moves. In a typical month, 8-12 champions change jobs. The AE reaches out within 48 hours with a congratulations message and an offer to help the champion evaluate tools for their new role. These conversations convert to qualified meetings at 35% because the champion already knows the product, trusts the rep, and is actively building their stack. The team generates $150K-$250K in pipeline per quarter from job change signals alone.

Recruiting Team Building Candidate Contact Pipelines

A tech recruiting team at a Series C startup uses LeadIQ to capture candidate contact information from LinkedIn Recruiter. When they identify a qualified senior engineer, LeadIQ provides the candidate's personal email address, which is more effective than InMail for initial outreach. InMail response rates for the team average 8%. Personal email response rates average 22%. The recruiter captures 40-50 candidates per day with verified contact data, compared to 12-15 per day with manual research. The time savings let each recruiter manage 50% more open requisitions. Monthly LeadIQ cost per recruiter: $79.

Pricing Analysis

LeadIQ offers a Free plan and three paid tiers. The Free plan includes 20 verified work emails per week, 10 mobile phone numbers per month, and 40 email address generations per month. It's enough for individual contributors testing the platform.

The Essential plan at $39/month per user adds unlimited prospecting with higher credit limits, CRM integration (Salesforce, HubSpot), and basic workflow features. The Pro plan at $79/month per user adds Scribe AI personalization, job change alerts, expanded monthly credits, and team management features. Enterprise pricing is custom with higher limits, advanced integrations, SSO, and dedicated support.

All plans include the Chrome extension for LinkedIn and Sales Navigator. Monthly contact capture credits vary by plan and reset monthly. The Essential plan provides sufficient credits for moderate prospecting (150-250 contacts per month). The Pro plan expands limits for power prospectors who capture 300+ contacts per month. Annual billing saves approximately 20% on all paid plans.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is LeadIQ's contact data?

LeadIQ reports email verification rates of 90%+ for verified business emails in their database. Direct dial phone number coverage is lower, typically 40-60%, reflecting the general scarcity of direct dial data across all providers. Accuracy is strongest for mid-market and enterprise contacts at well-known companies. Small company and startup contacts have lower coverage. The data is single-source, so total coverage is lower than waterfall enrichment tools.

Does LeadIQ work without Sales Navigator?

Yes. LeadIQ's Chrome extension works on regular LinkedIn profiles and search results. Sales Navigator provides better search filters, saved lead lists, and InMail credits that enhance the prospecting workflow. But LeadIQ's capture and enrichment features function on any LinkedIn profile page. Most serious prospectors pair LeadIQ with Sales Navigator for the best experience.

How does LeadIQ compare to Apollo?

Apollo is an all-in-one platform with its own prospecting database, built-in email sequences, a dialer, analytics, and enrichment. LeadIQ focuses specifically on LinkedIn-to-CRM capture with contact enrichment and AI writing. Apollo is better for teams wanting a single platform for prospecting and outreach. LeadIQ is better for reps who prospect primarily through LinkedIn and already use Outreach or Salesloft for sequencing.

Can LeadIQ enrich a CSV list in bulk?

LeadIQ supports bulk enrichment through CSV uploads, but the feature isn't the platform's core strength. Processing speed and coverage for bulk lists lag behind dedicated enrichment tools. For bulk list enrichment, Clay, FullEnrich, or Databar are better suited. LeadIQ's value is in the real-time LinkedIn capture workflow, not batch processing large datasets.

What is LeadIQ Scribe?

Scribe is LeadIQ's AI email writing feature available on the Pro plan ($79/month per user). It analyzes a prospect's LinkedIn profile, company, role, and recent activity to generate personalized email copy. Scribe produces subject lines, personalized openers, body text, and calls to action tailored to each individual prospect. The output quality is strong for first-touch outreach and saves SDRs 30-60 minutes per day on email personalization.
