The Bottom Line

Expandi is the right tool for agencies and power users who need LinkedIn automation with the lowest possible risk of account restrictions. The dedicated IP, human-like behavior patterns, and cloud-based execution are meaningfully safer than cheaper alternatives. For agencies managing multiple client accounts, this safety premium pays for itself by avoiding the business risk of a client's LinkedIn account getting restricted.

Individual reps who want basic connection request automation don't need Expandi's sophistication. Dripify at $39/month does the job for simpler use cases. The learning curve and price of Expandi only make sense if you're running complex multi-step campaigns with A/B testing and conditional logic.

Buy Expandi if you're an agency, power user, or enterprise team running LinkedIn outreach at scale and account safety is a priority. Buy Dripify or SalesRobot if you need basic LinkedIn automation at a lower price and can tolerate slightly higher account risk.

Expandi Overview

Expandi is the premium LinkedIn automation platform. It runs in the cloud (no browser extension needed), assigns a dedicated IP address per account, and mimics human behavior patterns to reduce the risk of LinkedIn account restrictions. For agencies and power users running LinkedIn outreach at scale, Expandi is the safest option available.

The platform supports smart sequences that combine connection requests, messages, profile views, and endorsements into multi-step campaigns. Dynamic personalization using prospect data (company, title, mutual connections) makes messages feel hand-written. A/B testing lets you optimize connection acceptance and response rates across campaign variations.

At $99/month per LinkedIn account, Expandi costs more than most competitors. Dripify starts at $39/month. SalesRobot at $99/month. The premium is for safety and reliability. Expandi's dedicated IP addresses and human-like activity patterns mean your LinkedIn account is less likely to get flagged or restricted. For agencies managing multiple client accounts, that protection is worth the extra cost.

The main drawback is complexity. Expandi's feature set is deep, and configuring smart sequences with conditions, delays, and A/B tests takes time to learn. Casual users who just want to send connection requests won't use 80% of the platform. Those users are better served by simpler tools like Dripify.

Pros & Cons

  • Best safety features for LinkedIn automationDedicated IP address per account, human-like behavior patterns (random delays, working hours, gradual ramp-up), and cloud-based execution make Expandi the safest automation tool for LinkedIn. Account restriction rates are significantly lower than browser-based alternatives.
  • Smart sequences with sophisticated logicBuild multi-step campaigns with conditional branching: if a prospect accepts the connection, send message A; if they don't after 3 days, try a different approach. This logic creates more natural outreach flows than simple linear sequences.
  • Dynamic personalization at scaleUse CSV data, LinkedIn profile fields, and custom variables to personalize every message. Image personalization lets you embed the prospect's name or company logo into visuals. The personalization depth keeps response rates high even at volume.
  • Cloud-based with no browser dependencyExpandi runs entirely in the cloud. Your computer doesn't need to be on, and there's no Chrome extension that could conflict with other tools or slow down your browser. Campaigns run 24/7 on Expandi's infrastructure.
  • Expensive per LinkedIn accountAt $99/month per LinkedIn account, costs add up fast. An agency managing 10 client accounts pays $990/month before any other tool costs. Dripify offers similar basic functionality at $39-$79/month per account.
  • Steep learning curve for advanced featuresSmart sequences, A/B testing, conditional logic, and webhook integrations are powerful but take time to master. New users report 1-2 weeks before they're comfortable building complex campaigns. The documentation is adequate but not exceptional.
  • Support response times can be slowMultiple users report support tickets taking 24-48 hours for initial response. For agencies with client-facing campaigns, slow support during a campaign issue creates risk. The knowledge base covers common scenarios but edge cases require human support.
  • LinkedIn still limits daily activity regardlessEven with Expandi's safety features, LinkedIn enforces daily limits on connection requests (approximately 100/week), messages, and profile views. Expandi won't let you send 500 connection requests per day. The tool optimizes within LinkedIn's limits, not around them.

Pricing Analysis

Expandi offers a single Business plan at $99/month per LinkedIn account (seat). There's no tiered pricing. Annual billing discounts are available (roughly 20% off). Agency plans with 5+ seats may qualify for volume discounts through direct negotiation.

The per-seat model means each LinkedIn account you want to automate requires a separate $99/month subscription. Agencies running campaigns for multiple clients should model total costs carefully. 10 client accounts at $99 each totals $990/month.

Expandi includes all features on every plan: smart sequences, A/B testing, dynamic personalization, webhooks, and analytics. There's no feature gating. The only variable is number of seats.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Expandi safe for LinkedIn?

Expandi is the safest LinkedIn automation tool available. Dedicated IP addresses, human-like behavior patterns, gradual activity ramp-up, and working-hours scheduling minimize account restriction risk. No tool can guarantee zero risk (LinkedIn prohibits all automation), but Expandi reduces it significantly compared to browser-based alternatives.

How much does Expandi cost?

Expandi costs $99/month per LinkedIn account. All features are included. Annual billing saves roughly 20%. Volume discounts are available for agencies with 5+ seats. There's no free plan, but a 7-day free trial is available.

Is Expandi better than Dripify?

Expandi is better for power users and agencies who need advanced safety features, conditional sequences, and A/B testing. Dripify is better for individuals and small teams who want basic LinkedIn automation at a lower price ($39-$79/month). If you're managing more than 3 LinkedIn accounts, Expandi's safety features justify the premium.

Can Expandi send connection requests automatically?

Yes. Expandi automates connection requests with personalized messages, profile views, endorsements, and follow-up messages. You set the daily limits and timing. The platform respects LinkedIn's weekly connection request limit (approximately 100/week) to maintain account safety.

Does Expandi work without Sales Navigator?

Yes. Expandi works with any LinkedIn account (free, Premium, or Sales Navigator). However, Sales Navigator provides better search filters for building target lists. Most Expandi users pair it with Sales Navigator for prospecting and use Expandi for automated outreach sequences.
