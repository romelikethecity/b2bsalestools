The Bottom Line

Clearbit Reveal is the best visitor identification tool for HubSpot users. The native integration means visitor intelligence flows into your CRM without configuration, enrichment data enables instant lead scoring, and the cost may be bundled into your existing HubSpot subscription. For the massive HubSpot install base, this is a no-brainer add-on that requires almost no decision-making.

For everyone else, the picture is murkier. Clearbit's richest company data is a genuine advantage over Leadfeeder and RB2B. But the post-acquisition focus on HubSpot integration means non-HubSpot users face platform risk. The pricing is less transparent, the standalone product roadmap is uncertain, and the long-term commitment to CRM-agnostic customers is unclear. If you're on Salesforce or another CRM, Leadfeeder is a safer long-term bet.

Buy Clearbit Reveal if you run HubSpot and want visitor identification with deep firmographic enrichment built into your CRM. Consider Clearbit if you need the richest company data available and are comfortable with the platform trajectory. Skip Clearbit if you want person-level identification (choose RB2B), if you need European coverage (choose Leadfeeder), or if you want end-to-end automation (choose Warmly).

Clearbit Reveal Overview

Clearbit Reveal identifies the companies visiting your website by matching IP addresses against Clearbit's company database. It's the same underlying technology as Leadfeeder and other IP-resolution tools, but Clearbit's advantage is the depth of company data attached to each identification. When Clearbit identifies a visiting company, you don't just get a name. You get firmographic details: employee count, revenue estimate, industry, technology stack, funding stage, and dozens of other attributes. This enrichment depth comes from Clearbit's core business as a data provider.

HubSpot acquired Clearbit in late 2023, and the product is being integrated into HubSpot's platform as part of the 'Breeze Intelligence' suite. For existing HubSpot customers, this is significant news. Clearbit Reveal capabilities are being baked directly into HubSpot, meaning visitor identification and enrichment data will live natively inside the CRM without requiring a separate tool or integration. The integration is still evolving, but the direction is clear: Clearbit becomes HubSpot's data layer.

The practical impact of the HubSpot acquisition is mixed. HubSpot users get Clearbit's company identification and enrichment at no additional cost (included in certain HubSpot tiers) or at reduced pricing. That's a strong value proposition. But non-HubSpot users face an uncertain future. Clearbit's standalone products are being absorbed into HubSpot, and the long-term availability of Clearbit for Salesforce-first or CRM-agnostic teams is unclear. This platform risk is worth factoring into any purchasing decision.

For pure visitor identification, Clearbit Reveal performs comparably to Leadfeeder on company matching. The differentiation is in what happens after identification. Clearbit attaches rich firmographic data that lets marketing teams score and route visitors based on company attributes, build targeted audiences from visitor segments, and personalize website experiences for different company profiles. If you're already on HubSpot and want visitor identification that feeds directly into your CRM and marketing automation, Clearbit Reveal is the natural choice.

Pros & Cons

  • Richest company data attached to each identificationEvery identified company comes with firmographic data that other visitor ID tools can't match: employee count, estimated revenue, industry classification, technology stack, funding history, and more. This data enables instant lead scoring and prioritization without additional enrichment steps. A marketing team can immediately segment visitors into 'enterprise target' vs 'too small' based on revenue data attached at identification time.
  • Native HubSpot integrationFollowing the acquisition, Clearbit data flows directly into HubSpot CRM without requiring integration setup or maintenance. Visitor identification, company enrichment, and contact data enrichment all operate natively within HubSpot's interface. For the 200,000+ companies using HubSpot, this eliminates the friction of managing a separate visitor ID tool and keeps all intelligence in one system.
  • Website personalization capabilitiesClearbit Reveal can customize what visitors see on your website based on identified company attributes. An enterprise visitor sees enterprise-focused messaging and case studies. A startup visitor sees startup pricing and relevant content. This dynamic personalization turns your website into a segmented experience without requiring the visitor to self-identify through forms. Conversion rate lifts of 10-30% are common with well-implemented personalization.
  • Included in HubSpot for many usersHubSpot is bundling Clearbit capabilities into its platform at no extra cost for certain subscription tiers. Teams already paying for HubSpot Marketing Hub Professional or Enterprise may get visitor identification without additional spend. Even for tiers that charge separately, the pricing is favorable compared to buying a standalone visitor ID tool plus a separate enrichment provider.
  • Company-level identification onlyLike Leadfeeder, Clearbit Reveal identifies companies, not individuals. You'll know that someone from a specific company visited your pricing page, but you won't know which person. For sales teams that need to contact a specific person immediately after a site visit, RB2B's person-level identification is more actionable. Clearbit compensates with richer company data, but the person-level gap remains.
  • Future uncertain for non-HubSpot usersThe HubSpot acquisition means Clearbit's standalone products are being folded into HubSpot's ecosystem. Non-HubSpot users can still access Clearbit's API, but investment in standalone products is declining. Teams on Salesforce or other CRMs should consider whether Clearbit will keep serving them long-term or whether a platform-neutral tool like Leadfeeder is a safer bet.
  • Pricing opacity after acquisitionClearbit's pricing has become less transparent since the HubSpot acquisition. Standalone pricing, bundled HubSpot pricing, and API pricing exist in overlapping tiers that require a sales conversation to sort out. The simplicity of RB2B's $99/month or Leadfeeder's published pricing is absent here. Budget planning requires talking to a HubSpot rep.
  • Identification coverage gaps on remote workersLike all IP-based identification tools, Clearbit struggles with remote workers on residential internet connections. As more B2B professionals work from home, the percentage of identifiable visitors decreases. Corporate VPN traffic is identifiable, but visitors on home WiFi with consumer ISPs often aren't. This is an industry-wide limitation, but it's important to set realistic expectations on match rates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Clearbit Reveal still available as a standalone product?

Clearbit's standalone products are being progressively integrated into HubSpot's Breeze Intelligence suite. The Clearbit API remains available for developers and non-HubSpot users, but new feature development is focused on the HubSpot-integrated version. Non-HubSpot users can still access Clearbit data through the API, but should monitor product announcements for changes to standalone availability. The long-term direction favors HubSpot-native usage.

How does Clearbit Reveal compare to Leadfeeder?

Both identify companies visiting your website using IP resolution. Clearbit attaches richer firmographic data (revenue, employee count, tech stack, funding) to each identification. Leadfeeder has stronger European coverage through the Dealfront platform and more mature visitor analytics features. Choose Clearbit if you're on HubSpot or need deep company data for lead scoring. Choose Leadfeeder if you sell into Europe or need a CRM-agnostic solution with proven filtering.

Does Clearbit Reveal work with Salesforce?

Clearbit has a Salesforce integration that syncs company and contact enrichment data. However, post-acquisition investment is focused on HubSpot integration. The Salesforce integration works but may receive fewer updates going forward. Salesforce-first teams should evaluate whether Clearbit's Salesforce support will remain strong or whether a CRM-agnostic tool like Leadfeeder is a more sustainable choice.

Can Clearbit identify individual people on my website?

Clearbit Reveal identifies companies, not individuals. The enrichment data helps narrow down likely contacts (you can filter by job title within identified companies), but it doesn't confirm which specific person visited your site. For person-level identification, look at RB2B. Clearbit's strength is attaching rich company data that enables scoring and routing, not individual identification.

What happens to my Clearbit subscription if I don't use HubSpot?

Existing Clearbit API customers retain access under their current agreements. New customer acquisition for standalone Clearbit is shifting toward HubSpot-bundled offerings. If you're evaluating Clearbit for the first time and don't plan to use HubSpot, consider the platform risk. The API will likely remain available, but product innovation will increasingly favor HubSpot-integrated features. Non-HubSpot users should have a backup plan.
