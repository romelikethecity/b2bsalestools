Regie.ai Overview

Regie.ai started as an AI content generation tool for sales teams. The original product helped reps write better cold emails, LinkedIn messages, and call scripts using AI. That foundation evolved into a full AI SDR platform as the company recognized the opportunity to automate not just the writing, but the entire outbound workflow. The pivot has been gradual, and the product reflects its origins. Regie.ai's content generation capabilities remain strong, with the AI SDR automation layered on top.

The platform now offers Auto-Pilot agents that can run outbound sequences autonomously. You define the ICP, provide messaging guidelines, and the AI handles prospect identification, email generation, sequence management, and follow-up. The content quality benefits from years of training on sales messaging data. Emails tend to be well-structured and on-brand, especially when you invest time in the content guidelines during setup. The AI learns from your team's existing top-performing emails, which gives it a head start on voice and tone matching.

Regie.ai also maintains a Co-Pilot mode where the AI assists human reps rather than replacing them. Co-Pilot suggests email drafts, rewrites messages in real time, and recommends next-best actions. This hybrid approach appeals to teams that want AI assistance without full autonomy. You can start with Co-Pilot and graduate to Auto-Pilot as trust builds, which is a practical deployment path that most pure AI SDR tools don't offer.

Pricing is custom and not publicly disclosed. Based on market reports, expect plans starting in the mid-four-figures per month for Auto-Pilot capabilities. Co-Pilot pricing for individual reps is lower. The lack of pricing transparency is frustrating in a category where buyers want to compare costs quickly. Regie.ai's strength is content quality. Their weakness is that the AI SDR functionality is still catching up to purpose-built competitors like 11x and Artisan who designed for full autonomy from day one.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Regie.ai an AI SDR or an AI writing tool?

Both. Regie.ai started as an AI writing tool for sales teams and expanded into autonomous AI SDR capabilities. Co-Pilot mode is an AI writing assistant that helps human reps. Auto-Pilot mode is a fully autonomous AI SDR. You can use either or both. The content generation heritage means Regie.ai's email quality is strong relative to purpose-built AI SDR competitors.

How does Regie.ai compare to 11x?

11x is purpose-built for autonomous outbound with deeper prospect research. Regie.ai offers better content generation and a flexible Co-Pilot mode for teams that want AI assistance without full autonomy. 11x is more expensive ($5K+ vs custom pricing). Choose 11x for maximum autonomy. Choose Regie.ai for superior content quality and a graduated approach to AI outbound.

Does Regie.ai work with Outreach or Salesloft?

Yes. Regie.ai integrates with both Outreach and Salesloft, plus HubSpot and Salesforce. The AI content engine can feed into your existing engagement platform, generating emails and sequences that execute through your current workflow. This makes Regie.ai a practical layer on top of your existing stack.

How long does it take to see results with Auto-Pilot?

Budget 2-3 weeks for setup and content calibration. The AI needs to learn your voice, brand guidelines, and messaging preferences before it produces consistently good output. Early emails will require review and feedback. After 30-45 days of tuning, most teams report the Auto-Pilot output meets their quality standards. Results in terms of meetings booked start appearing in month two.

Can I use Regie.ai just for the AI writing features?

Yes. Many customers use only Co-Pilot for AI-assisted email writing without enabling Auto-Pilot. The Co-Pilot mode integrates with your existing workflow and helps reps write better emails faster. You don't need to commit to autonomous outbound to get value from Regie.ai's content capabilities.
