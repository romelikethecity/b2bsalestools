The Bottom Line

SalesRobot occupies a sensible middle ground: stronger safety than Dripify, multi-channel capability like Skylead, responsive support that's better than both, and a price point ($99/month) that's competitive with the market. It's a credible alternative for teams that want LinkedIn automation with good account protection and don't need Expandi's most advanced features.

The main risk is maturity. SalesRobot is newer and less battle-tested than Expandi. The feature set is solid but still evolving. Teams that need enterprise-grade features, deep analytics, or extensive integration options should stick with Expandi. Teams that value support quality and multi-channel capability over feature depth will find SalesRobot delivers.

Buy SalesRobot if you want a balanced LinkedIn + email automation tool with strong safety and great support. Buy Expandi if you need the most battle-tested LinkedIn automation with the deepest feature set. Buy Dripify if budget is the primary concern and you can accept simpler safety features.

SalesRobot Overview

SalesRobot positions itself as the safe LinkedIn automation tool. The platform uses multiple browser fingerprints and sophisticated behavior patterns to mimic human activity on LinkedIn, reducing the risk of account restrictions. At $99/month for the starter plan, it's priced between Dripify and Expandi while aiming to match Expandi's safety standards.

The platform supports both LinkedIn and email outreach. Smart sequences combine connection requests, LinkedIn messages, email sends, and follow-ups with conditional branching. The multi-channel approach is similar to Skylead's, but SalesRobot puts more emphasis on the safety layer. Multiple browser fingerprints per session, randomized timing, and gradual activity warm-up are standard.

SalesRobot is a newer entrant in the LinkedIn automation space. The product has improved rapidly, with regular feature releases and responsive customer support. Users consistently highlight the support team as a differentiator. For agencies and teams that need help configuring campaigns, that support quality matters.

The trade-off is maturity. SalesRobot's feature set is solid but not as deep as Expandi's. Campaign templates, analytics, and integration options are still catching up. Teams that need advanced A/B testing, detailed reporting, or enterprise features will find gaps. Teams that prioritize safety and customer support over feature depth will appreciate the platform.

Pros & Cons

  • Strong safety features with multiple fingerprintsSalesRobot uses multiple browser fingerprints to mimic different browsing sessions, combined with randomized timing and gradual activity ramp-up. The safety approach is more sophisticated than Dripify and comparable to Expandi's dedicated IP model.
  • Multi-channel LinkedIn + emailSequences combine LinkedIn actions (connection requests, messages, profile views) with email sends. Conditional logic routes prospects based on behavior. The multi-channel capability competes with Skylead at a similar price point.
  • Responsive customer supportUsers consistently rate SalesRobot's support highly. Response times are faster than Expandi's. The team helps with campaign setup, troubleshooting, and optimization. For teams new to LinkedIn automation, hands-on support reduces the learning curve.
  • AI-powered message writingBuilt-in AI generates personalized connection request messages and follow-ups based on prospect profiles. The AI writing is a time-saver for reps who struggle with LinkedIn copywriting. Quality is adequate for initial outreach.
  • Newer tool with less market validationSalesRobot launched more recently than Expandi or Dripify. The user community is smaller, and there are fewer case studies, reviews, and third-party resources available. Early adopters get strong support but less peer guidance.
  • UI could be more polishedThe interface is functional but not as refined as Dripify's clean design or Expandi's power-user layout. Navigation can feel cluttered when managing multiple campaigns. The team is iterating quickly, so this may improve.
  • Template library is limitedFewer pre-built campaign templates and sequence frameworks compared to Expandi. Teams need to build more campaigns from scratch. The AI writing feature partially offsets this, but a richer template library would accelerate onboarding.
  • Integration ecosystem still developingNative integrations are limited compared to more established tools. Zapier and webhooks cover most use cases, but teams wanting direct CRM or engagement platform connections may need workarounds.

Pricing Analysis

SalesRobot offers two main plans. Starter at $99/month includes LinkedIn automation, email outreach, smart sequences, and AI message writing. Pro at $179/month adds advanced analytics, team management, priority support, and higher daily limits. Enterprise pricing is custom.

All plans include cloud-based execution and multi-fingerprint safety features. Annual billing saves approximately 20%. A 14-day free trial is available on both plans.

The pricing is competitive with Expandi ($99/month) while including email outreach that Expandi doesn't offer. Compared to Skylead ($100/month), SalesRobot offers similar multi-channel capability with stronger safety emphasis.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SalesRobot safer than Expandi?

SalesRobot's multi-fingerprint approach is comparable to Expandi's dedicated IP model. Both offer strong LinkedIn account protection. Expandi has a longer track record, which gives more confidence in long-term safety. SalesRobot offers slightly better support for configuring safety settings per account.

Does SalesRobot include email outreach?

Yes. SalesRobot combines LinkedIn automation with email sequences in the same campaign workflow. Conditional logic routes prospects between LinkedIn and email based on their behavior. This is similar to Skylead's multi-channel approach.

How does SalesRobot compare to Dripify?

SalesRobot ($99/month) is more expensive than Dripify ($39-79/month) but offers stronger safety features, email outreach, and better customer support. Choose Dripify for basic LinkedIn automation on a budget. Choose SalesRobot if safety, multi-channel outreach, or hands-on support are priorities.

Can SalesRobot write LinkedIn messages for me?

Yes. SalesRobot includes an AI message writer that generates personalized connection requests and follow-up messages based on prospect data. The AI considers the prospect's title, company, and LinkedIn activity. Quality is good enough for initial outreach, though high-value prospects may warrant hand-crafted messages.

Does SalesRobot work with Sales Navigator?

Yes. SalesRobot imports prospects from LinkedIn Sales Navigator search results, standard LinkedIn search, and CSV uploads. Sales Navigator provides better targeting filters for building prospect lists.
