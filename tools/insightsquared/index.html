The Bottom Line

InsightSquared is the best sales analytics visualization tool in the revenue intelligence category. The interactive dashboards, pipeline waterfall charts, and rep performance scorecards turn CRM data into actionable insights faster than any competitor. For sales managers drowning in spreadsheet reporting, InsightSquared delivers immediate value with minimal setup.

The honest concern is the product's trajectory. The Mediafly acquisition in 2022 has slowed visible feature development. The competitive landscape has shifted: CRM-native reporting has improved, and tools like BoostUp now offer AI-driven analytics at accessible price points. InsightSquared's sweet spot (better-than-CRM analytics at less-than-Clari pricing) is getting squeezed from both directions.

Buy InsightSquared if you need strong data visualization and sales analytics at a moderate price point and you're comfortable with the product's current feature set. Buy BoostUp if you want AI-driven forecasting and pipeline management at a similar price. Buy Clari if forecast accuracy and deal intelligence are the priorities. Ask Mediafly hard questions about the product roadmap before signing a multi-year commitment.

InsightSquared Overview

InsightSquared is a revenue analytics platform that fills the gap between basic CRM reporting and enterprise revenue intelligence tools like Clari. The product focuses on sales analytics, interactive dashboards, and pipeline forecasting with a strong emphasis on data visualization. For sales teams frustrated by Salesforce's native reporting limitations but not ready for a $50K+ revenue intelligence platform, InsightSquared has historically been a popular middle-ground option.

The platform connects to your CRM (Salesforce or HubSpot) and pulls pipeline, activity, and revenue data into pre-built and customizable dashboards. Pipeline analytics, rep performance scorecards, and conversion funnel reports come out of the box. The interactive visualizations are a strength. Dragging through pipeline waterfall charts, drilling into stage-by-stage conversion rates, and filtering by any dimension feels more like a BI tool than a typical sales analytics product.

Forecasting capabilities exist but are less AI-driven than Clari or Aviso. InsightSquared uses statistical models and historical trends rather than deep machine learning. The forecasts are useful for identifying directional trends but don't provide the deal-level health scoring or AI confidence intervals that purpose-built forecasting platforms deliver. Think of it as enhanced analytics with forecasting features, not a forecasting platform with analytics.

The company's trajectory requires honest acknowledgment. InsightSquared was acquired by Mediafly in 2022, and the product roadmap has been less visible since the acquisition. Customer reviews from 2024-2025 mention slower feature development and questions about long-term product direction. The core analytics and visualization capabilities remain solid, but prospective buyers should evaluate Mediafly's plans for InsightSquared during the sales process.

Pros & Cons

  • Best-in-class data visualization for sales analyticsInsightSquared's interactive dashboards are the product's standout feature. Pipeline waterfall charts, conversion funnel views, and trend analyses are visually clear and easy to explore. Sales managers can drag, filter, and drill into data without building custom reports. The visualization quality exceeds what Clari, BoostUp, or native CRM reporting provides.
  • Faster time to value than enterprise alternativesCRM connection and initial dashboard setup takes 1-2 weeks. Pre-built report templates cover the most common sales analytics use cases (pipeline by stage, rep activity, conversion rates, forecast vs. actual). Teams start getting insights from day one instead of waiting 4-8 weeks for an enterprise platform to fully deploy.
  • Strong rep and manager scorecardsPerformance dashboards break down rep activity, pipeline creation, conversion rates, and quota attainment into clear scorecards. Managers can compare reps side-by-side and identify coaching opportunities. The scorecards are more visual and interactive than what most CRMs provide natively.
  • Forecasting lacks AI sophisticationInsightSquared uses statistical models and trend analysis for forecasting, not the machine learning models that Clari, Aviso, or Gong Forecast deploy. Deal-level health scoring and AI confidence intervals are absent. The forecasting is directionally useful but won't match the accuracy of AI-native platforms. If forecasting accuracy is your primary need, InsightSquared isn't the right tool.
  • Product direction uncertain after Mediafly acquisitionMediafly acquired InsightSquared in 2022 and the product roadmap has been harder to track since. Feature releases have slowed. Customer reviews mention uncertainty about the product's long-term direction. Evaluate carefully whether InsightSquared's current capabilities will be supported and improved over the next 2-3 years.
  • Limited activity capture compared to competitorsInsightSquared relies more heavily on CRM data than tools like Clari or Aviso, which capture email and calendar activity automatically. If your reps don't consistently log activities in Salesforce, InsightSquared's analytics will have gaps. The platform doesn't supplement CRM data with its own activity capture layer.
  • Positioning is squeezed from both sidesSalesforce's native analytics have improved significantly. Clari and BoostUp have become more accessible to mid-market buyers. InsightSquared occupies a narrowing space between better-than-CRM and full-revenue-intelligence. The value proposition that was clear in 2019 is harder to articulate in 2026.

Use Cases

Sales Manager Replacing Spreadsheet Reporting

A sales manager at a 200-person company is tired of exporting Salesforce data to Excel every Monday morning to build pipeline reports. She deploys InsightSquared and connects it to Salesforce in one afternoon. By the next Monday, she has interactive dashboards showing pipeline by stage, rep activity scorecards, and week-over-week pipeline changes. The 3 hours per week she spent building spreadsheet reports drops to 15 minutes reviewing dashboards. Total cost: a fraction of what Clari would charge, and she's getting value within a week.

VP Sales Tracking Conversion Rate Trends

A VP Sales notices quota attainment declining over 2 quarters but can't pinpoint why from Salesforce reports alone. InsightSquared's conversion funnel analytics reveal the problem: stage 2 to stage 3 conversion dropped from 45% to 28% over the past 6 months. He drills into the data by rep, segment, and lead source. The analysis shows that leads from a new marketing channel convert at half the rate of established channels. He redirects $150K of marketing budget to higher-converting channels. The entire analysis takes 2 hours in InsightSquared versus what would have taken a week of spreadsheet work.

RevOps Analyst Building Custom Sales Dashboards

A RevOps analyst builds custom dashboards for different stakeholders: a CRO dashboard showing quarterly forecast vs. actual, a VP Sales dashboard showing pipeline by region, and a frontline manager dashboard showing rep-level activity metrics. InsightSquared's dashboard builder lets her create all three in 2 days with drag-and-drop visualizations. Each dashboard auto-refreshes from Salesforce data. Stakeholders access their views through bookmarked links instead of requesting ad hoc reports from RevOps.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is InsightSquared still actively developed?

InsightSquared is maintained by Mediafly following the 2022 acquisition. Feature development has slowed compared to pre-acquisition pace. The core analytics and visualization features remain solid. Ask Mediafly directly about the product roadmap, recent feature releases, and investment plans during your evaluation. This is the most important question to get a clear answer on.

How does InsightSquared compare to Clari?

InsightSquared is primarily a sales analytics and visualization tool. Clari is a revenue intelligence platform with AI-driven forecasting, deal health scoring, and pipeline inspection. InsightSquared has better data visualization. Clari has better AI, better forecasting accuracy, and more mature deal-level insights. Choose InsightSquared for analytics. Choose Clari for forecasting. They serve different primary needs.

Does InsightSquared work with HubSpot?

Yes. InsightSquared integrates with both Salesforce and HubSpot. The HubSpot integration supports pipeline analytics, activity reporting, and conversion tracking. Feature parity between the two CRM integrations is close, though the Salesforce integration has more historical development behind it.

Can InsightSquared replace a BI tool?

For sales-specific analytics, InsightSquared can replace Looker or Tableau dashboards focused on pipeline and rep performance. It's faster to set up and easier for non-technical users. For cross-functional analytics (marketing, finance, product data), you still need a general-purpose BI tool. InsightSquared is scoped to sales and revenue data.

How does InsightSquared's forecasting work?

InsightSquared uses statistical models and historical trend analysis to project pipeline outcomes. It analyzes stage-by-stage conversion rates, deal velocity, and seasonal patterns to estimate quarterly revenue. The approach is mathematical but not AI-driven. You won't get deal-level health scores or AI confidence intervals. It's more like intelligent reporting than predictive intelligence.
