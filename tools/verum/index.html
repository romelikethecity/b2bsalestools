What is Verum?

Verum is a b2b contact & company data tool. Done-for-you data enrichment and cleaning service. Combines 50+ sources with human QA. Per-record pricing means you only pay for what you use, but there's no self-serve option.

Best for: Midmarket sales teams (5K+ records) that need clean, enriched data without managing another platform

Best For

Pros & Cons

  • 50+ data sources with human QA on every record
  • 93% email deliverability guarantee
  • Full-service: cleaning, enrichment, and validation in one engagement
  • Not a platform. No login, no dashboard, no real-time API
  • Minimum $2,000 project size isn't ideal for very small teams
  • Turnaround is 24-48 hours, not instant

Key Features
