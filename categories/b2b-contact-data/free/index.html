Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best free B2B data tool?

Apollo.io. 10,000 email credits per month, basic sequencing, and a dialer. No credit card required, no time limit. It's the most generous free tier in B2B data.

Are free B2B data tools accurate?

Free tiers generally have the same data quality as paid plans. Apollo's free data accuracy is identical to its paid tiers. The limits are on volume, not quality.

Can you run cold outbound on just free tools?

Yes. Apollo (free data + sequences) and HubSpot (free CRM) give you a complete outbound stack at zero cost. Add Fireflies for free call recording. The limits are real but workable for 1-2 reps.
