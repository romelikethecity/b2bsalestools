Frequently Asked Questions

Best B2B data tool for a 20-person sales team?

Apollo at $79-119/user/mo. Large database, built-in engagement, and enough features to avoid buying separate tools. Cognism if you sell into Europe and need GDPR compliance.

When should an SMB upgrade from Apollo to ZoomInfo?

When you need intent data, advanced technographics, or your team exceeds 50 reps. Until then, Apollo's data quality and value are hard to beat at the mid-market level.

How many data credits does a mid-size team need?

Budget 500-1,000 credits per rep per month for active prospecting. Apollo's Professional plan ($79/mo) includes enough credits for moderate outbound volume.
