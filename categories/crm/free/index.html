Frequently Asked Questions

Is HubSpot CRM really free?

Yes, free with no time limit. Unlimited users, up to 1 million contacts, deals, tasks, email tracking, and meeting scheduling. The paid features are real upgrades, not unlocking basics.

What CRM features require a paid plan?

Advanced automation, custom reporting, sequences beyond basic, predictive lead scoring, and advanced permissions. HubSpot Starter at $15/mo adds the most impactful upgrades.

Is Zoho CRM Free good?

Zoho CRM Free supports up to 3 users with basic CRM features. It's more limited than HubSpot Free but works for very small teams already in the Zoho ecosystem.
